How does it work?

Want the whole school to know who you’re crushing on but still keep control? Here’s how Crush Drop makes your life easier and guarantees some drama.

First, you send your secret message through the app, easily.
Then, an admin checks everything to make sure it’s clean and approves the messages to avoid any surprises.
Once approved, your crush can appear in the published feeds visible to everyone — but you can stay anonymous if you want to keep it secret.
It’s simple, fast, and it raises the tension throughout the whole school!
